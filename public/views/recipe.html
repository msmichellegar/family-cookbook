Maree's Lasagne

Recipe of: Maree Garrett

Cooking time: 1 hour

This lasagne has been cooked by Barrons for over a decade. A particularly good recipe for secretly feeding children green vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of fresh lasagne sheets
  • 1 tub of cottage cheese
  • 1 tub of cream cheese
  • 1 bunch of spinach

Directions

1. Put the lasagne sheets in a dish
